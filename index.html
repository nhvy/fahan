About Fahan Foundation

Our mission is to promote social justice, alleviate poverty and support sustainable development through education.

Social Justice

We amplify equal access to education and opportunities for underserved communities.

Poverty Alleviation

We support indigent pupils with essential school materials and targeted assistance.

Sustainable Development

We invest in education as a long-term strategy for resilient families and thriving communities.
